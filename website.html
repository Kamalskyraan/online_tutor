Welcome to our Truck Ledger App, a smart digital solution created to make lorry trip and expense management easy, accurate, and stress-free. The app allows lorry owners and drivers to record every trip, fuel cost, repair expense, advance, payment, and vehicle details in one secure place, removing the need for notebooks and loose papers.

Users can track trip history, mileage, earnings, and expenses in real time, helping you clearly understand profit and performance for each vehicle. Driver and owner details are neatly stored, making coordination smooth and transparent. With simple screens and organized reports, the app helps you avoid errors, save time, and make better business decisions. Overall, the Truck Ledger App turns daily lorry operations into a clean, reliable, and fully digital system that keeps your transport business running smarter and more efficiently.

Assign Trips

Trips assigned to you will appear here with all the important details in one place. You can view and manage trip information such as starting point, destination, driver, vehicle, load, and expected earnings. This section helps you track each journey easily, monitor progress, expenses, and payments, and stay updated without any confusion. With assigned trips clearly listed, both lorry owners and drivers can stay organized and focused on every trip.

Completed Trips

View a full history of all completed trips with clear details like route, driver, vehicle, expenses, and earnings, helping you easily track performance, verify records, and understand your lorry’s actual income from every journey.

Report

Add all your expenses as a record to clearly see what was spent during each trip, helping you track fuel, repairs, tolls, and other costs in an organized way. This makes it easy to analyze where most money is going. It also supports better budgeting and cost control for future trips.

Find Owners

Locate nearby lorry owners and view their details. This feature helps you quickly connect with owners for trip planning, vehicle hiring, or support. It also makes coordination easier by providing clear and reliable contact information.

Overall Expenses

Keep a complete log of all expenses occurring during a trip. This gives owners a clear view of total spending, including fuel, repairs, and other charges. It helps in tracking profitability and ensuring accurate settlement for every trip. With this, financial control becomes more transparent and reliable.

Running a lorry business isn’t just about moving goods from one place to another. It’s about managing drivers, tracking vehicles, handling trip details, watching fuel and toll expenses, and keeping an eye on earnings, all at the same time. Our app brings every moving part together in one organized space, giving you a clear view of lorry owners, drivers, vehicle models, trips, and expenses without the usual hassle.

No paperwork, no confusion, no guesswork. Track every trip, manage costs, and stay in control with organized records that actually make sense. Whether you’re on the road or off it, this is where your lorry business stays sorted.
